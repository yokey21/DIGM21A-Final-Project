Music Production

A hobby I developed over the past few years is music production.I enjoy collaborating with others and creating music together. I am part of two music groups known as Young Horizons and Fiji Gang. While Young Horizons mainly produces alternative chilled music, Fiji Gang is a hip-hop group.

Young Horizons

Me and my buddy Kennion creates the group known as Young Horizons. We started in high school and although we have not published it, we have been producing music ever since. I focus on the production and piano, while my friend provides his amazing vocals and guitar skills. Please enjoy one of our first singles "It Hurts So Bad" below!

Fiji Gang

Another group which began in high school, we began my creating rap songs as a joke, but it quickly became something much more. Me and another member known as "Noa the Boa" are planning on releasing a full EP this year. So stay tuned!
